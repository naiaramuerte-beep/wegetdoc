# Paddle Refund Policy Requirements

## Key findings from Paddle's own refund policy:

1. **Default position**: "all Transactions are non-refundable and non-exchangeable" (unless required by law)
2. **EU/EEA/UK consumers**: 14-day statutory right to withdraw
3. **South Korea**: 7-day unconditional right to cancel
4. **Brazil**: 5-day unconditional right to cancel
5. **Discretionary refunds**: Paddle may issue refunds at its discretion

## What Paddle requires from sellers:
- Refund policy must NOT contain qualifiers or exceptions
- Refund policy MUST specify exact number of days for refund window
- Must match Paddle's refund policy (14 days for EU, which is the standard)

## Problem with our current policy:
Looking at our current policy, it says "14 days, no questions asked" which seems clean.
BUT the section 5 (Subscriptions) says "Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period" - this could be seen as a qualifier.
Also section 4 says "access to premium features will cease immediately" - this could be seen as a condition.

## Solution:
- Simplify to match Paddle's exact language
- Remove any conditional language
- State clearly: 14 days, full refund, period.
- Reference Paddle's refund policy directly since Paddle handles all refunds as MoR
