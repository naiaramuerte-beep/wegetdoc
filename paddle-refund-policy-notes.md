# Paddle Refund Policy - Key Points

## What Paddle requires from suppliers:
1. No qualifiers or exceptions in the refund policy
2. Specify exact number of days for the refund window
3. Must match Paddle's refund policy

## Paddle's actual policy:
- All transactions are non-refundable by default (section 1.1)
- EU/EEA/Switzerland/UK: 14-day statutory right to withdraw (section 2.2)
- Turkey/Israel: 14-day right to withdraw (section 2.3)
- South Korea/Brazil/China/Canada: 7-day right to cancel (section 2.4)
- Singapore: 5-day right to cancel (section 2.5)
- Discretionary refunds: within 14 days of transaction date (section 7)
- Refunds processed within 14 days of approval using same payment method
- Access to product ceases upon refund

## What our refund policy MUST say:
- 14-day refund window (no exceptions, no qualifiers)
- No conditions like "only if you haven't used the service"
- No language like "at our discretion" or "may be eligible"
- Clear, unconditional: "You can request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase"
- Must mention that access ceases upon refund
- Must mention how to request (contact support)
